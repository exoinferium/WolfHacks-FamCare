Emergency Guide

Know when to seek immediate medical help

🚨 Call Emergency Services Immediately If:

💔

Chest Pain

Sudden chest pain, pressure, or tightness

🧠

Neurological

Sudden confusion, severe headache, or loss of consciousness

🫁

Breathing Issues

Severe difficulty breathing or shortness of breath at rest

🩸

Severe Bleeding

Uncontrolled bleeding or major injuries

🌡️

Extreme Fever

Fever above 104°F (40°C) or fever in infants

⚠️

Severe Reactions

Allergic reactions with swelling, difficulty breathing, or rash

🏥 Visit Urgent Care or ER Within Hours If:

🤕
Injury or Trauma

Possible fractures, head injuries, or major falls

🥵
High Fever

Fever 101-103°F that doesn't respond to medication

🤢
Severe Symptoms

Persistent vomiting, severe abdominal pain, or diarrhea

👁️
Vision/Hearing Loss

Sudden vision or hearing changes

💊
Medication Issues

Suspected overdose or severe medication reaction

🩹
Wound Concerns

Deep cuts, puncture wounds, or signs of infection
